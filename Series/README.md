# Series

## Purpose
Maintain all things related to a series.  For the purposes of this application a movie with no sequels would be a tv series with 1 episode.

## Definition

A series is a unique body of work that is not tied to others.

To use a real world example

### A Single Movie
The movie Casablanca is a movie without any sequels.  This would be a series with 1 season and 1 episode of type `movie`

### A Movie With Sequels
The movies Back To The Future consist of 3 parts.  This would be a series with 1 season and 3 episodes of type `movie`

### TvSeries and Movies intermixed
The television show Futurama has movies that should play in between episodes.  Each movie would be considered an episode along with each broadcasted episode.  Depending on business needs a movie be considered a season, or share it's season with other movies 

Thus a series can be a collection of seasons, movies
